# Product Sheet — Everyday Savings (Reference Sample)

> Synthetic product information for the AgentForge reference example.

## Everyday Savings Account

- **Interest rate:** 3.5% AER variable, calculated daily and paid monthly.
- **Minimum opening balance:** $1.
- **Withdrawals:** unlimited, no notice period.
- **Eligibility:** available to individual customers aged 18+ who have completed
  KYC identity verification.
- **Fees:** no monthly account fee.

## Everyday Saver Plus

- **Interest rate:** 4.1% AER fixed for 12 months.
- **Minimum opening balance:** $5,000.
- **Withdrawals:** limited to 3 per year; early withdrawal forfeits accrued bonus interest.
