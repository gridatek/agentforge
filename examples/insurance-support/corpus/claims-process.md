# AutoGuard Claims Process

How a policyholder files an auto claim and how AutoGuard handles it.

## Reporting a claim

- A claim must be **reported within 30 days** of the incident.
- To open a claim, the policyholder submits a **claim form**, **photos of the
  damage**, and, for theft or collision, a **police report**.

## Handling and timelines

- An **adjuster contacts the policyholder within 3 business days** of the claim
  being opened.
- AutoGuard issues a **claim decision within 15 business days** of receiving all
  required documents.
- Approved repairs are paid to the repair shop directly, or reimbursed to the
  policyholder, minus the applicable deductible.

## Total loss

- A vehicle is declared a **total loss** when the cost to repair it exceeds its
  value. Total-loss claims are paid at the vehicle's **actual cash value (ACV)**
  minus the applicable deductible.
- Actual cash value reflects the vehicle's market value immediately before the
  loss, accounting for age, mileage, and condition.

## Deductibles

The deductible is the amount the policyholder pays before coverage applies; it is
subtracted from any claim payment. See the coverage summary for deductible amounts.
