# AutoGuard Personal Auto Policy — Coverage Summary

This summary describes the coverages, limits, and deductibles of the AutoGuard
personal auto policy. It is a reference example for AgentForge and does not
describe a real insurance product.

## Liability coverage

- **Bodily injury liability:** $100,000 per person and $300,000 per accident.
- **Property damage liability:** $50,000 per accident.
- Liability coverage applies when the insured driver is at fault for injuries or
  damage to others. It does not cover the insured's own vehicle.

## Collision and comprehensive

- **Collision** covers damage to the insured vehicle from an impact with another
  vehicle or object. The collision **deductible is $500**.
- **Comprehensive** covers non-collision damage such as theft, fire, hail, or
  hitting an animal. The comprehensive **deductible is $250**.

## Additional coverages

- **Uninsured/underinsured motorist:** $100,000 per accident, covering injuries
  caused by a driver with no or insufficient insurance.
- **Rental reimbursement:** up to **$40 per day** for a maximum of **30 days**
  while a covered vehicle is being repaired.
- **Roadside assistance** (towing, jump-start, lockout) is included on the
  **Premium** tier only and is optional on other tiers.

## Tiers

AutoGuard is sold in three tiers — Basic, Standard, and Premium. Higher tiers add
roadside assistance and lower the rental-reimbursement waiting period; coverage
limits and deductibles above are the same across tiers unless endorsed otherwise.
